zoom in with „+“, zoom out with „ - “

Show Nearest

You have to allow your browser to return your current location to the server in order to activate this button.

Map (on the right):

The „Show Nearest“ button shows your current location in the middle of the map, marked with the cross. All visible dots on the map are nearby stations of Vienna public transport lines (bus, tram or metro), reachable within 10 minutes of walking. A click on any station moves you to the tabulator „Stations“, showing the name of the station that you have marked.

Information window (on the left):

All nearby stations shown in the map are listed in the information window, ordered by the kind of transport (bus, tram or metro). For each station both directions of the line are shown (by the name of the respective endstation) and the remaining minutes until the next vehicle arrives are listed next to the name of the line. A coloured button next to the line shows if the line is running at good service (green; up to 2 minutes delay), delayed service (yellow; more than 2 minutes up to 5 minutes delay) or if the service is interrupted (red; more than 5 minutes delay).

A click on a station listed in the information window will lead you immediately to the tabulator „Stations“ (see chapter 2 „Stations“).

Show Map Center

Map (on the right):

The „Show Map Center“ button loads the nearest stations of the location on the map that you have selected (they are coloured in amber). The stations farer away than about 10 minutes of walking time are coloured in dark red. A click on any station moves you to the tabulator „Stations“, showing the name of the station that you have marked.

Information window (on the left):

All nearby stations shown in the map in amber are listed in the information window, ordered by the kind of transport (bus, tram or metro). For each station both directions of the line are shown (by the name of the respective endstation) and the remaining minutes until the next vehicle arrives are listed next to the name of the line. A coloured button next to the line shows if the line is running at good service (green; up to 2 minutes delay), delayed service (yellow; more than 2 minutes up to 5 minutes delay) or if the service is interrupted (red; more than 5 minutes delay).

If the number of stations for one station exceeds the size of the information window, a scrollbar on the right side of the information window enables scrolling through the list of station names of all modes.

Show Car2Go

Map (on the right):

The „Show Car2Go“ button loads the nearest available Car2Go vehicles in the area that was selected on the map, showing them with green dots on the map. A click on any green dot opens an information window with the details about the car (exact address, shape of interior, shape of exterior, fuel level, vehicle-nuber and license plate number). Another click removes the window with additional information.

Stations

Information about any station in Vienna is shown. The station can be selected in the textfield of „Station Name“. A list of proposed station names, containing the letters typed in the textfield will be shown in a dropdown menue. After the selection of the desired station name, a click on the button „Show“ right to the textfield will show all information about the station.

Map (on the right):

The selected station is marked by a green button in the map. A station may contain many platforms. All platforms of a selected station are marked by blue buttons on the map. At each platform a public transport vehicle stops in some direction. The exact location of the platform is shown in the center of the map, if selected in the map or by selecting the „Show Platform“ button in the information window.

Information window (on the left):

For each platform the following information is shown:

  • The number of the passing line

  • The direction the passing line (by the name of the final destination station)

  • The service status of the passing line

    • green: good service - less than 2 minutes delay

    • yellow: delayed service - more than 2 minutes up to 5 minutes delay

    • red: interrupted service - more than 5 minutes delay

  • Remaining minutes until the arrival of the next 3 vehicles

The platforms are ordered by mode of public transport (Bus, Tram and U-Bahn).

If the number of platforms for one station exceeds the size of the information window, a scrollbar on the right side of the information window enables scrolling through the list of platform names of all modes.

Lines

Map (on the right):

All stations of the desired line number selected in the information window is highlighted in the map by yellow/amber dots. A click on any station in the map leads you immediately to the tabulator „Stations“.

Information window (on the left):

The desired line number can be typed in the texfield „Line Name“. A list of proposed line number names, containing the letters typed in the textfield will be shown in a dropdown menue. After the selection of the desired line number, a click on the button „Show“ right to the textfield will show information about the selected line.

For each line the following information is shown:

  • All station names of the line are listed from one end station to the other

  • At the right side of each listed station name the „service dot“ shows the service of the line at this station, according to ist colour:

    • green: good service - less than 2 minutes delay

    • yellow: delayed service - more than 2 minutes up to 5 minutes delay

    • red: interrupted service - more than 5 minutes delay

A click on any station name of the listed names will lead you immediately to the tabulator „Stations“

Status Updates

The tabulator „Status Updates“ shows all interruptions that occur currently in the public transport of Vienna.

If there are more interruptions, it is possible to select one interruption by clicking on the title on the information window. Then the location(s) of the related stations will be highlighted by amber/yellow points in the map.

A description of the interruption is available on the information window.
